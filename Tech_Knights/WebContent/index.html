Not-in-my-back-yard (NIMBY) is a common culture followed by most people across the world, i.e. as long as their waste is collected from their homes and offices, they do not care what happens to it or how it impacts the environment.

The wet waste is either composted on-site or it is collected and sent to our biogas partner. All the dry waste is sorted into 16 categories by our trained field staff and prepared for the recycling industry. For example, the plastics are sorted into around 10 different categories and paper is sorted into over five categories as per the requirement of our recycling partner.

There are still certain categories of waste as sanitary waste which cannot and should not be recycled. This material is sent to a government approved site.
